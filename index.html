Our Story

Started by One Teacher with a Big Vision

BrightPath began as a simple idea: give boys in our school community a safe, structured place to go after school hours. What started as a small program at one school has grown to serve students at three nearby schools.

Founded and run entirely by a passionate teacher out of his own pocket, this program is proof that one dedicated person can make a lasting difference. With your support, we can reach even more students.

What We Offer

A Complete After-School Experience

Our program is designed to support the whole student — mind, body, and character.

📚

Academic Support

Homework help, tutoring, and study skills that help students stay on top of their schoolwork and build confidence in the classroom.

⚽

Sports & Activity

Physical activity, team sports, and healthy competition to keep students active, focused, and learning the value of teamwork.

🌟

Life Skills & Mentorship

Real-world guidance on responsibility, leadership, communication, and growing into a confident and kind young man.
